About Me

I'm Kevin, nice to meet you!

I am an incoming 4th year at the University of California, San Diego studying Human-Computer Interaction, Computer Science, and Design. For the summer, I am a UX Design Intern for ResMed!

As a teenager, I would roam online forums and create for people. Having these people tell me thanks through a screen was so reassuring, but design gives me the opportunity to understand. To actually see who I’m creating for and why. I believe a design is so much more than the end product -- it’s all people, all the minute details, all the collaboration that makes design truly rewarding.

In my free time, I love to:

Follow the NFL - I still root for the Chargers!

Cook - try to, at least.

Stay active - if I can sweat, I'll probably like it.

Discover new music genres - I've been listening to post-rock!
